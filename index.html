SETTLO

Core Utility

On-chain payroll settled in stablecoins.
Pay global teams in USDC instantly. No banks. No borders. No delays.

Stream salaries, lock yield, and manage treasury from one protocol.

Settlo is a permissionless payroll protocol built on Arc Network. Organisations of any size can stream wages, schedule batch disbursements, and auto-compound idle treasury into high-yield vaults, all settled in USDC with sub-second finality and zero counterparty risk.

Settlo · Stablecoin · 2025
  • ✦ Real-time salary streaming — pay per second, not per month
  • ✦ Dual-vault yield engine — Flexible 8.5% APY or Locked 14.2% APY
  • ✦ Multi-sig treasury — threshold approvals and on-chain audit trail
  • ✦ Arc Network native — gas-free USDC with instant cross-border settlement
